Privacy Notice

We collect the minimum necessary personal data. Here’s what, why and for how long.

Who we are

Razorline Labs (sole proprietor) — Netherlands. Contact: hello@razorlinelabs.nl.

What we process

  • Contact details you provide (name, email, company) via email or meeting booking.
  • Booking details via Calendly (date/time, basic intake answers).
  • Email metadata as part of correspondence (sender, timestamps).
  • Server logs (standard web logs incl. IP and user-agent) for security and error analysis.

Purposes & legal bases

Scheduling & communication
Contractual necessity (GDPR art. 6(1)(b)).
Security & diagnostics
Legitimate interest (GDPR art. 6(1)(f)).
Legal obligations
Accounting retention (GDPR art. 6(1)(c)).

Processors / third parties

  • Calendly (scheduling) — may set functional cookies.
  • Apple iCloud Mail — email processing.
  • GitHub Pages — static site hosting.
We use EU locations or appropriate safeguards and sign DPAs where required.

Retention

  • Booking/contact data: up to 12 months after last interaction or as needed for an engagement.
  • Billing/administration data: 7 years (statutory).
  • Server logs: up to 90 days unless needed for security investigation.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection and portability. Email hello@razorlinelabs.nl. You may lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

Cookies & analytics

We do not use marketing cookies. The Calendly embed may set functional cookies. If we add privacy-friendly analytics later, we’ll update this notice.
