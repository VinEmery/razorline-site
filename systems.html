Example systems

Patterns we specialise in

Each implementation is unique, but the same building blocks show up repeatedly: events, agents, RAG, humans and AI Ops.

Support Agent with Memory & Escalation

Designed for teams drowning in repetitive incoming requests. This system triages, answers and escalates tickets.

  • Detect intent and classify tickets
  • Use RAG to pull knowledge from docs and past threads
  • Generate responses for low-risk tickets
  • Escalate high-risk or unclear tickets to humans with full context
  • Log interactions for search, QA and compliance

Sales Follow-up Agent Suite

Built for B2B teams who lose deals because of slow or inconsistent follow-up.

  • Listen for new leads from forms, CRM or events
  • Score and segment leads based on your rules
  • Draft personalised outreach and propose times
  • Update CRM records and tasks automatically
  • Surface exceptions for sales to handle directly

Finance Reconciliation Pipeline

LLM-assisted reconciliation pipeline for invoices, payments and internal ledgers, with anomaly detection and reporting.

  • Ingest invoices, payment exports and ledger data
  • Structure and normalise fields with LLM assistance
  • Match, flag anomalies and propose adjustments
  • Generate reconciliation reports
  • Provide an auditable trace of decisions

Can we build something outside these patterns?

Likely yes — as long as it is a system with clear inputs, transformations and outputs, and not just a “chatbot”. During the Blueprint we determine if your use case fits our strengths.
