About

Razorline Labs in one paragraph

A small, technical boutique focused on one thing: AI systems that quietly run real operations in the background.

Founder: Vincent Emery

I started in DevOps and platform engineering, working on CI/CD, cloud infrastructure and reliability for software teams. Over time, it became obvious that the next step was not more tooling — it was intelligent systems layered on top of existing infrastructure.

Razorline Labs is my vehicle to focus purely on that: applied AI systems and automation architecture for organisations that care about production quality, auditability and long-term maintainability.

I work primarily with teams in the Netherlands and EU, as a ZZP consultant. I prefer hybrid engagements where we work closely with your existing engineers rather than trying to replace them.
