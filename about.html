Razorline Labs in one paragraph

Founder: Vincent Emery

I started in DevOps and platform engineering, working on CI/CD, cloud infrastructure and reliability for software teams. Over time, it became clear that the next step was not more tooling — it was intelligent systems layered on top of existing operations.

Razorline Labs exists to build those systems: applied AI, multi-agent architectures, RAG pipelines and automation that handle real business processes.

I work primarily with companies in the Netherlands and EU as a ZZP consultant. I prefer hybrid engagements where we integrate closely with your existing engineers rather than trying to replace them.
